>east
Petting Zoo

A cheerful open-air enclosure filled with friendly animals. Pygmy goats trot around nibbling at visitors' shoelaces, while a pair of fluffy rabbits hop lazily near a hay bale. A feed dispenser is mounted on a post. An info plaque is posted by the gate. The main path is back to the west.

You can see hay bale, info plaque, bag of animal feed, feed dispenser, pygmy goats, rabbits here.

>take feed
Taken.

>drop feed
Dropped.
The pygmy goats spot the bag of feed and rush over! They crowd around, bleating excitedly, and devour the corn and pellets in seconds. The smallest goat looks up at you with big grateful eyes.
