Zoo Entrance

You stand before the wrought-iron gates of the Willowbrook Family Zoo. A cheerful welcome sign arches over the entrance, and a small ticket booth sits to one side. A sturdy iron fence runs along either side of the gates. The main path leads south into the zoo grounds.

You can see staff keycard, welcome sign, ticket booth, iron fence, zoo brochure, zoo map, backpack here.

>south
Main Path

A wide gravel path winds through the heart of the zoo. Colorful direction signs point every which way. A park bench sits beside the path. To the east, the petting zoo. To the west, the aviary. A staff gate blocks the path to the south. The entrance is back to the north.

You can see staff gate, direction signs, flower beds, souvenir penny, park bench, lunchbox, zookeeper here.

>examine signs
Arrow signs: PETTING ZOO (east), AVIARY (west), EXIT (north).

>east
Petting Zoo

A cheerful open-air enclosure filled with friendly animals. Pygmy goats trot around nibbling at visitors' shoelaces, while a pair of fluffy rabbits hop lazily near a hay bale. A feed dispenser is mounted on a post. An info plaque is posted by the gate. The main path is back to the west.

You can see hay bale, info plaque, bag of animal feed, feed dispenser, pygmy goats, rabbits, zookeeper here.

>examine goats
Three pygmy goats hoping you have food.
*DING DONG* "Attention visitors! The Willowbrook Family Zoo will be closing in three hours. Please make sure to visit all exhibits before closing time!"

Aviary

You step inside a soaring mesh dome. Brilliantly colored parrots chatter from rope perches, and a toucan eyes you curiously from a branch overhead. A small waterfall splashes into a stone basin. An info plaque hangs near the entrance. The gift shop is to the west. The main path is back to the east.

You can see toucan, waterfall, rope perches, aviary plaque, parrot here.
